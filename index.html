Profile

We are an independent team of strategic, ideas-led creatives based in Brisbane, Australia.  We specialise in brand identity, creative direction, digital design and campaigns, creating new brands and refreshing old ones.

We work with ambitious brands, organisations and individuals, that look to challenge the expected, explore possibility and strive for excellence.

Through our collaborative process we work hard to get to the root of our clients problems, uncovering insights and identifying opportunities to accelerate growth and ultimately create a brand which will endure and stand apart.

As a studio, we believe in keeping things simple – deliver strategic creativity by removing the unnecessary to bring out the essential, with a clear destination in mind.

We regularly collaborate with a network of experienced strategists, web developers, writers, editors, photographers, filmmakers and animators. As a result, we are able to connect our clients to a wide range of skilled creatives and help them build a team based on the specific needs of each project.

Folkal
Brand Identity, 2022

Folkal is a marketplace designed to help people make a conscious choice when buying sunglasses. Folkal curate fashion-forward styles and rank them by their impact on the planet. A joint venture between creative, content and media agency Edge and global eyewear giant, Safilo, the marketplace features an industry-first sustainability rating system that allows consumers to quickly and easily see which frames are better for the planet. Our task was to work with Edge’s strategy to create a positive, direct and eye-catching design system. A typographic approach was created to enable the marketplace to deliver emotive, factual or playful headlines to drive sales and knowledge. The language based approach is supported by a bold minimal palette and a circular graphic system, to talk to the earth and eyes. All the assets ultimately combine to ensure the sustainability of the brands and their products are the focus of communications.

Perennial Better Future
Brand Campaign, 2022

Perennial Better Future is an investment company focusing on the next generation of authentic Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) investing. Working with marketing agency Better, the objective of this campaign was to create awareness and educate people of the Perennial Better Future brand with two key target audiences – financial advisers and direct investors –while also positioning Perennial Better Future as the preeminent pure-play ESG investment manager in Australia. We developed a distinctive look and feel with colours that are vibrant, future-focused and positive, combined with human-centric empowering images of people in a shaft of light that talk to a better and brighter future. Five months after the campaign started, the levels of familiarity of the financial advisers who recalled seeing the campaign increased by 100%, and the Perennial Better Future product consideration within that target audience increased by 112.5%.
